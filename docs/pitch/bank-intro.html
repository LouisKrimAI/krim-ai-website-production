Introducing KrimOS

The operating system
for regulated AI.

AI in banking fails for one reason: intelligence and governance built separately, connected afterwards. KrimOS builds them as a single system — every agent action validated before it executes, every decision on a single auditable record, everything running inside your perimeter.

01
Validated before it acts
Every agent action passes through 33 pre-execution validators. Pass, amber, or fail. Nothing executes on an amber or a fail.
02
Auditable by construction
Deterministic, reproducible decision records. The same inputs produce the same output, every time. Readable by an examiner or a board.
03
Never leaves your walls
Deployed inside your infrastructure. Your data stays yours. The intelligence compounds inside the perimeter where it already lives.
RBI MRMF
2026
RBI's draft model risk framework (consultation closes 24 July 2026) mandates independent validation of every model, explainability for every material decision, and genuine human control over every AI agent. It applies to every commercial bank, NBFC, co-operative, and credit information company in India. KrimOS was built to these principles before the draft existed.
