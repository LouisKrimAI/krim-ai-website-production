Head of Collections

More accounts.
Fewer conversations.
Falling recovery.

Your team is working harder on a growing portfolio, but the tools have not kept pace. Dialler lists built on lagging data. Scripts that never adapt to the borrower. Strategies that respond to arrears instead of preventing them.

What KrimOS delivers
Recovery-ranked priority

The contact queue is ordered by recovery probability, refreshed continuously as the system sharpens its read of each borrower's behaviour. The highest-value recoverable accounts reach the right agents at the right moment.

Recovery potential sets the queue.
Outreach that fits the borrower

Contact strategy, channel, timing and resolution approach matched to each account's history. The borrower who responds to a text at 9am is different from the one who answers the phone on a Saturday.

Effort lands where recovery is likeliest.
Resolve before it worsens

Payment intent signals are monitored continuously, and the right resolution offer triggers before the account deteriorates further. The system reads the window of willingness and acts inside it.

Escalation becomes the exception path.

The shift: From reactive, volume-driven collections to a model where the system surfaces the account, the moment and the approach, and your team works where it creates the most value.
