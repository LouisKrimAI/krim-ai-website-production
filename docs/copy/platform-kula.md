## 2 · Intent becomes a governed plan
EYEBROW: HOW IT WORKS
H2: Ask. Suggest. Act. Learn.
COPY: Describe what you want — "bring down missed payments next quarter." Kula proposes a plan: segments, steps, co-workers, guardrails. Nothing has happened yet. You review it; on approval it runs — every action validated. What it learns sharpens the next plan.
PRESENTATION: the signature device — type intent, watch a governed, validated plan assemble (Ask→Suggest→Act→Learn), with a clear "awaiting your approval" beat. It never skips the human.
